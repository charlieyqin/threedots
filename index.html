FAQ

  • Help! "threedots can't be opened because it is from an unidentified developer?"

    Right-click on the app and choose "Open" and that error will go away forever.

  • Where's Google login?

    Google login doesn't currently work, so if you use a Google account to log in to Asana just open asana.com in Safari before opening threedots.

  • Is this an official Asana app?

    Nope, just something I hacked together on a weekend.

  • How does it work?

    Pretty simple - I just load Asana in a webview, and inject a little JavaScript (you can see it here).

  • Are you stealing my Asana password?

    Nope - but if you want to be sure, just log into Asana.com in Safari before you open threedots and you won't have to log in at all. You can also check out the source code for yourself.

  • What's the catch?

    There's no catch - I just made something I found useful and felt like sharing :)

    You do have to have OS X Yosemite or later, since threedots uses a framework that doesn't exist in earlier versions.

  • What doesn't work?

    Google login doesn't currently work, so if you use a Google account to log in to Asana just open asana.com in Safari before opening threedots.

    Dropbox works, but you can't attach files from Box, Google Drive, or your computer.

    Be warned that if Asana changes how their product works in a major way, threedots might stop working.
